  1. When you check "Remember Me", a persistent token is generated
  2. The token is stored in the database and sent as a cookie to your browser
  3. Even after session expiration, the cookie authenticates you automatically
  4. Tokens are more secure than storing username/password in cookies
  5. You can invalidate tokens by logging out
